How it works
  1. The controller creates one JobSet with two replicatedJobs: a leader (1 pod) and workers (N pods). JobSet starts them together (gang scheduling).
  2. Each worker runs real Monte Carlo sampling and streams its partial counts to the leader over the stable JobSet pod DNS (a headless service).
  3. The leader aggregates all partials into a live running estimate: π = 4 × inside / total.
  4. Hit Kill on any worker pod (in the topology panel) to delete it. JobSet's failurePolicy recreates all child Jobs — the whole group restarts.
